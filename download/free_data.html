Free datasets from NCAR/RDA

A number of datasets which can be used to generate the input files of WRF-Var can now be download directly from the NCAR/RDA web site:

  • Only a limited number of datasets are available online
  • The data is free to all users to download

Available GRIB datasets: (for input to WPS)

  • NCEP Final Analysis (FNL from GFS) (ds083.2): 1 degree resolution, every 6 hours
    • http://rda.ucar.edu/datasets/ds083.2
  • NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis (ds090.0): 2.5 degree resolution, every 6 hours
    • http://rda.ucar.edu/datasets/ds090.0
  • NCEP GRIB GDAS (ds083.0): 2.5 degree resolution, every 12 hours. - you may be able to use it if your entire domain stays in one hemisphere.
    • http://rda.ucar.edu/datasets/ds083.0
  • NCEP Eta/NAM (ds609.2): 40 km resolution, every 6 hours.
    • http://rda.ucar.edu/datasets/ds609.2

Available observational datasets:

  • Surface and upper-air observational data (in ADP format) is also available to users. See the next section for instructions on how to convert ADP format to LITTLE_R.
    • Surface and Ship data (ds464.0)
    • Upper Air data (ds353.4)

Converting alternative observation formats to LITTLE_R:

  • Once you have ADP data on your machine, you can use the script adp_to_little-r.csh (inside the latest FETCH TAR file - after Jan 21, 2004) to convert it to LITTLE_R input format.
    This converter is NOT maintained by the WRFDA group, and should be used with caution.
  • For users with MADIS-formatted data, the WRF Data Assimilation group maintains the MADIS2LITTLER converter.
