WRF users workshop

The WRF Users' Workshop is conducted annually (typically in late June). The next workshop will be held from June 15–19, 2015. Registration information can be found at this page.

The purpose of the workshop is to provide a forum for WRF users to share new developments, present research highlights, and discuss issues of interest to both the users and developers.
