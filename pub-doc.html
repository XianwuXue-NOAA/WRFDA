Publications utilizing the WRFDA system

The WRFDA section of NCAR MMM has published over 30 papers and technical manuals since its inception in 2003. In addition, there have been more than 100 papers published on research done using the WRFDA system. An incomplete list is provided below. If you used WRFDA in a peer-reviewed research paper and do not see it listed below, please or contact wrfhelp, and we will be sure to add it to the list.
