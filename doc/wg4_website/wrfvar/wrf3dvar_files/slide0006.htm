Decisions about first version of 3DVAR (continued)
Formulation
•In gridpoint space (as opposed to observation space)
•Formulations in gridpoint and observation spaces are not always equivalent.
•Evidence that condition number (rate of convergence) might be much better in observation space
•Not difficult to change spaces later
• Analyze increments, not full field.
•No explicit balance constraints (no Jc term); no physical constraints
•Map factors supplied by Standard Initialization and used in initial version
•“square-root” preconditioning
•Adoption of outer/inner iteration structure
