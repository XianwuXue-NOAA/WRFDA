Institutions utilizing the WRFDA system

WRFDA is being used (or has been recently used) in some capacity at close to 100 (or more!) universities and research centers worldwide. We have tried to maintain a list below, along with some selected publications. Because WRF and WRFDA are a unified system, it is quite hard to know how many users are making use of WRFDA, so this likely represents an incomplete list. If you notice any errors or omissions, please or contact wrfhelp.

  • Operational WRFDA systems
  • Research users of WRFDA
